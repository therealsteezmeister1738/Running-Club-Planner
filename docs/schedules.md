# Recurring Run Schedules

## Weekly Group Runs

### Tuesday Evening - Social Run
- **Time:** 6:00 PM
- **Distance:** 3-5 miles
- **Pace:** All paces welcome (no-drop policy)
- **Meeting Point:** City Hall Plaza
- **Typical Route:** Downtown Loop or variation

### Thursday Morning - Tempo Run
- **Time:** 6:00 AM
- **Distance:** 5-7 miles
- **Pace:** Moderate to hard effort
- **Meeting Point:** Riverside Park
- **Route:** River Trail

### Saturday Morning - Long Run
- **Time:** 7:00 AM (8:00 AM in winter)
- **Distance:** 8-15+ miles (varies by training season)
- **Pace:** Easy conversational pace
- **Meeting Point:** Trailhead Parking
- **Route:** Rotates weekly - check scheduled runs

### Sunday Recovery Run
- **Time:** 8:00 AM
- **Distance:** 3-4 miles easy
- **Pace:** Very easy, conversational
- **Meeting Point:** Coffee Shop on Main St
- **Route:** Casual neighborhood loop
- **Post-run:** Coffee and socializing

## Seasonal Adjustments

- **Summer (Jun-Aug):** Saturday runs start at 7:00 AM to avoid heat
- **Winter (Dec-Feb):** Saturday runs start at 8:00 AM, Tuesday runs may move indoors if icy
- **Daylight Saving:** Evening run times may shift to maintain daylight

## How to Join

Just show up! No registration required. Introduce yourself to the group.

First time? Arrive 10 minutes early to meet the run leader and get route details.

## Weather Policy

- Runs happen rain or shine
- Canceled only for: lightning, ice storms, or dangerous conditions
- Check this repo or show up - we'll post cancellations here if needed
