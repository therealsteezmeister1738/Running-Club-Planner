# River Trail - 10K

**Distance:** 6.2 miles / 10.0 km  
**Elevation Gain:** 120 ft / 37 m  
**Route Type:** Out-and-back  
**Surface:** Paved trail  
**Difficulty:** Easy-Moderate

## Overview

Scenic out-and-back along the river on a dedicated multi-use trail. Mostly flat with one gentle climb at the turnaround point. Popular with runners and cyclists.

## Directions

1. Start at Trailhead Parking (Mile 0)
2. Head north on River Trail (paved, obvious path)
3. Pass mile markers 1, 2, and 3
4. Turnaround at the Blue Bridge (Mile 3.1)
5. Return same route to trailhead

## Key Features

- **Scenery:** River views entire way, wildlife (herons, ducks), forested sections
- **Traffic:** Low vehicle traffic (dedicated trail), moderate bike/pedestrian traffic
- **Shade:** 70% shaded by trees - great for summer
- **Water fountains:** Trailhead, Mile 2.5 (seasonal - Apr-Oct)
- **Bathrooms:** Trailhead parking area
- **Parking:** Free lot at trailhead (40 spaces, fills up weekends by 8am)

## Safety Notes

- Trail is isolated in sections - recommend running with buddy at dawn/dusk
- Watch for cyclists, especially on weekends
- Can be icy in winter - trail is not cleared
- Spotty cell service between miles 1.5-2.5
- Bring water in summer - only 2 fountains on route
