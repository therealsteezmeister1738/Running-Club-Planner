# Hill Training Loop

**Distance:** 4.5 miles / 7.2 km  
**Elevation Gain:** 450 ft / 137 m  
**Route Type:** Loop  
**Surface:** Road  
**Difficulty:** Hard

## Overview

Challenging loop designed for hill training. Features three significant climbs with recovery flats in between. Great for building strength and preparing for hilly races.

## Directions

1. Start at Community Center Parking
2. Warm-up flat 0.5 miles on Valley Road
3. **Hill 1:** Climb Summit Drive (0.3 mi, 120 ft gain)
4. Recovery descent and flat 0.7 miles
5. **Hill 2:** Climb Ridge Road (0.4 mi, 150 ft gain)
6. Recovery loop through neighborhood 1.0 mile
7. **Hill 3:** Climb Highland Ave (0.5 mi, 180 ft gain)
8. Cool-down descent 1.1 miles back to start

## Key Features

- **Scenery:** Residential neighborhoods, city views from top of hills
- **Traffic:** Low - mostly residential streets
- **Shade:** Partial
- **Water fountains:** Community Center (start/finish only)
- **Bathrooms:** Community Center (during business hours)
- **Parking:** Community Center lot

## Safety Notes

- Hills have no shoulder - run facing traffic
- Can be slippery when wet (steep sections)
- Limited lighting - avoid after dark
- Full cell service
- Bring water - no fountains on route

## Training Tips

- Do this loop once for maintenance, 2-3 repeats for serious hill training
- Focus on effort, not pace, on the climbs
- Use downhills for active recovery, not sprinting
