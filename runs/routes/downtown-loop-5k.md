# Downtown Loop - 5K

**Distance:** 3.1 miles / 5.0 km  
**Elevation Gain:** 85 ft / 26 m  
**Route Type:** Loop  
**Surface:** Road (sidewalks)  
**Difficulty:** Easy

## Overview

Perfect beginner-friendly loop through downtown. Flat terrain with good lighting for evening runs. Passes by several coffee shops and the waterfront.

## Directions

1. Start at City Hall Plaza (Main St & 1st Ave)
2. Head east on Main St for 0.5 miles
3. Turn right on River Road, follow waterfront path for 1.2 miles
4. Turn right on Park Ave, climb gentle hill for 0.4 miles
5. Turn right on 5th St, return to downtown for 0.6 miles
6. Left on Main St back to City Hall Plaza (0.4 miles)

## Key Features

- **Scenery:** Waterfront views, downtown architecture, park
- **Traffic:** Moderate - use sidewalks, watch for cars at intersections
- **Shade:** Partial (trees along waterfront)
- **Water fountains:** City Hall Plaza, Riverside Park (mile 1.8)
- **Bathrooms:** City Hall (business hours), Riverside Park
- **Parking:** Street parking near City Hall, public garage on 2nd Ave

## Safety Notes

- Well-lit entire route - safe for evening runs
- Heavy pedestrian traffic weekends 10am-2pm
- Watch for cyclists on waterfront path
- Full cell service throughout
